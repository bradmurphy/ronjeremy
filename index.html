How do you become an icon?

Many people have gone down in history for their talent, beliefs or simply the way they chose to live their life. The path they took to greatness may not have always been an easy one, but it was one they wouldn't dare think to regret.

Ron Jeremy Hyatt as he spent his youth growing up in Queens, New York.

Ron attended Queens College where he earned a Bachelor's degree in theater as well as a Master's degree in special education.

He taught special education classes and was a regular substitute at numerous schools in New York City until he quit to pursue an acting career.

His defining moment came when his ex-girlfriend sent a photo of Ron to Playgirl Magazine, the same magazine which he would later pose for.

Ron Jeremy's past may be one which is subject to scrutiny and criticism, but one cannot deny his passion and drive for success. It is a trait to be admired, and Ron De Jeremy wants to encourage the people to have the same fearlessness as Mr. Jeremy himself.

rum & lime

For a refreshing drink grab a large lime and cut off the ends. Cut the lime in half continuously until there are 4 pieces. Add lime pieces and two tablespoons of powder sugar into the glass. Use a pestle to squeeze the juice out of lime pieces. Fill the glass with crushed ice and add 2 ounces of Ron De Jeremy and stir.

fizzle & a spritz

Pour Ron De Jeremy rum and cola into a glass full of ice. Squeeze a lime over the top for a burst of extra flavor. Stir gently and let your taste buds enjoy.

twister

For sweet pick me up, shake and strain the ingredients into a tall glass full of crushed ice. Add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice and garnish with slice of blood orange. Best served with a straw.
